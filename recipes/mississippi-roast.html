Mississippi Roast

A yummy keto roast with minimal ingredients

Let the flavors envelope you as your taste buds enjoy the extraordinary flavors

Makes 12 servings!

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds chuck roast meat
  • 1 packet ranch dressing mix
  • 1 packet au jus gravy mix
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 4-5 pepperoncini peppers

Steps

  1. Put instant pot on saute mode
