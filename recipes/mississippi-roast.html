Mississippi Roast

A yummy keto roast with minimal ingredients

Let the flavors envelope you as your taste buds enjoy the extraordinary flavors

Makes 12 servings!

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds chuck roast meat
  • 1 packet ranch dressing mix
  • 1 packet au jus gravy mix
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 4-5 pepperoncini peppers
  • 1 chopped onion is optional (however will increase carbs)

Steps

  1. Put instant pot on saute mode
  2. brown meat
  3. add packets of au jus, ranch, and peppers
  4. 1 stick of butter (chopped)
  5. instant pot pressure cook on high for 90 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
