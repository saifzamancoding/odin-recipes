Butter Chicken

A yummy indian dish

Let the flavors envelope you as your taste buds enjoy the extraordinary flavors

Makes 12 servings!

Ingredients

  • 14 oz canned tomatoes; 2 TBsp tomato paste (keto option)
  • garlic
  • 2 teaspoon minced ginger
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon Garam Masala
  • 1 teaspoon Ground cumin
  • 1 pound chicken (better with skin on/bone in/thigh), if frozen add 2 mins cooking time.
  • 4 oz butter
  • 4 oz heavy cream (or coconut milk)
  • half cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 chopped onion is optional (however will increase carbs)

Steps

  1. Put instant pot on saute mode
  2. brown meat
  3. Put all ingreients into an Instant Pot in the order listed, EXCEPT for the butter, cream and 1 teaspoon of the garam masala, mixing the sauce well before you place the chicken on top of the sauce. If it's frozen, push it into the sauce a bit so it defrosts better
  4. Close the cooker and set for 10 mins on high, and let it release pressure naturally for 10 minutes. After that, release all remaining pressure. Open up the pot and remove the chicken carefully and set asideBlend together all the ingredients, preferably using an immersion blenderAdd the cut up butter, cream, cilantro, and garam masala and stir until well incorporated.
  5. It's best to let the sauce cool just a little before adding the butter and the cream. Adding it into the boiling sauce will make your sauce very thin. If that happens, just put it in the fridge for a little and let it thicken up. It should be thick enough to coat the back of a spoon Take out half the sauce and freeze for later or store in the fridge for 2-3 days Add the chicken back in and heat through. Break it up into smaller pieces if you need but don't shred it. Serve over rice, or zucchini noodles
  6. Enjoy!
